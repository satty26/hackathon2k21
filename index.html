ABOUT HACKTHON 2k21

A great idea requires a healthy environment to be turned into a reality. E-Cell at IIIT Pune comes forth with its flagship event - HACKATHON - a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers or anyone involved in software development collaborate and work on problems that itch their brains. We are glad to offer you an opportunity to connect with like-minded people and combine your enthusiasm levels to come up with authentic software solutions.

EVENT STARTS ON

April 17, 2021

THEMES

ANDROID APP DEVELOPMENT

Want to delight android users around you by coming up with intriguing android app solutions to make their lives easier? This is a hands-on experience to explore your potential in the field of android development with intensive coding skills.

FULL STACK WEB DEVELOPMENT

Enough of the user experience! Get onto the other side of the line. Now is a chance to build an effective website environment using all possible languages and frameworks of web development with added business logic and user mindset.

FRONT END WEB DEVELOPMENT

Test your expertise in creating user-friendly interfaces for user-data interaction using front end technologies. Contribute towards a safe as well as intuitive user experience by effectively combining your skills in design, technology and coding.
